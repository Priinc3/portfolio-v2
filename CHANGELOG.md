## [0.2.0] — 2025-05-14

### Changed
- **Typography Update**: Replaced Syne, DM Mono, and Instrument Serif with Plus Jakarta Sans, JetBrains Mono, and Bricolage Grotesque for significantly improved readability and a more modern, unique tech aesthetic.

### Technical Notes
- Switched to 'Plus Jakarta Sans' for primary display and body text due to its high legibility at all sizes.
- Integrated 'JetBrains Mono' for technical labels and mono text, providing a professional developer feel.
- Used 'Bricolage Grotesque' for stylistic accents, maintaining a unique and "alive" visual character.
